About Me

I am Mhmad Mostafa Mansour, a certified Syrian Deck Officer with 46 months of experience at sea, currently serving as a Second Officer. I have served onboard Bulk Carrier and General Cargo vessels, with a strong commitment to maritime ethics, human rights, and international cooperation.

Mission

To represent Syrian seafarers officially and professionally on the global maritime stage, advocate for their rights, highlight their hardships, and build legal, humanitarian, and institutional bridges with international maritime organizations.

Official Initiative

I propose the opening of a communication and coordination point in Tartous, Syria, under my name, to assist, represent, and support Syrian seafarers locally and internationally. This includes direct reporting, case follow-up, and professional development support.
